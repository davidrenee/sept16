Her Story

We met at Pope John Paul while attending summer school for grade 11 physics. My first impression of him was that he was funny and I noticed an uncanny similarity between his voice and that of Eric Forman from That 70's Show. We became friends but when summer school ended we didn't keep in touch. The following summer, I went back for grade 12 physics and to my surprise, David was there as well! He had the same humour and we instantly became friends again. This time we were sure to exchange phone numbers and planned to keep in touch. We kept in touch throughout first year of University but eventually lost contact.

Fast forward 5 years, I randomly get a text one day from a number I did not recognize. It read "Hi it's David!" Being the popular girl that I am, I did not know which David it was at first but after a few texts, I quickly remembered who he was. We made plans to hang out at Go For Tea and when we saw each other again, we instantly hit it off.

Fast forward another 5 years, David became my best friend. After countless laughs, a few tears, multiple vacations, becoming first time homeowners, and parents to our little Bella, I will be able to marry my best friend this fall.
